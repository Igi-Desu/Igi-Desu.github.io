UNITY C#

PAC-MAN 3D

Based on well know game - Pac-Man, but this one is not a simple pacman with just one map. By going to the side, player can go to next face of a cube.
Different colored ghosts, have different pathfinding algorithms, with purple one having A* pathfinding.
I had to come up with an idea, how to make gameplay on a cube, but also make pathfinding work despite environment being harsh for that kind of algorithms.
